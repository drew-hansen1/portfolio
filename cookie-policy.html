Cookie Policy

Effective date: December 8, 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile device) when you visit a website. They are commonly used to make websites work efficiently and to provide information to the site owner.

2. How We Use Cookies

This website uses cookies solely for basic analytics purposes through Google Analytics. We do not use cookies for advertising, marketing, or profiling.

Cookies help us understand:

  • How visitors find and navigate the website
  • Which pages are visited most often
  • General usage patterns and performance

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Analytics cookies (Google Analytics) Collect aggregated information about how visitors use the website Session and persistent (as defined by Google)

No essential, functional, marketing, or advertising cookies are used beyond analytics.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Google Analytics sets and reads cookies on our behalf. These cookies are subject to Google's own privacy and cookie policies. We do not control how Google uses this information.

5. Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View what cookies are stored on your device
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Block cookies from specific websites
  • Block cookies entirely

Please note that disabling cookies may affect how analytics are recorded but will not prevent basic access to the website.

You can also opt out of Google Analytics tracking by using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on or following instructions provided by Google.

6. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology or legal requirements. When updates are made, the "Effective date" above will be revised.
